FAQ

What counts as proof?

A screenshot of your NABA National confirmation or your logged-in member profile page is perfect.

Is membership really free for students?

Yes, student membership is free. Sign up on the official NABA site, then submit our form.

How long until I’m added?

We batch new members frequently. Expect a confirmation within 24–48 hours during the semester.

Need help?

We’re happy to walk you through the process if anything’s unclear.
