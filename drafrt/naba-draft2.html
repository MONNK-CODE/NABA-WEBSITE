About Us

NABA UIUC creates community, mentorship, and professional opportunities for Black and underrepresented students pursuing careers in accounting, finance, analytics, and business. We host workshops, company events, case competitions, and service initiatives while supporting our members from their first meeting through graduation.

Our Mission

To uplift, educate, and connect students with the resources and networks needed to launch meaningful careers — and to increase opportunities for Black professionals in accounting and allied fields.
