About Us

NABA UIUC is a student chapter of the National Association of Black Accountants. We connect students with career opportunities, professional development, and a strong community across accounting, finance, consulting, data, and related fields.

Our Mission

To lift as we climb—expanding the pipeline of Black talent, providing mentorship and resources, and advocating for representation in business. We host networking events, technical workshops, case competitions, and community service initiatives throughout the year.

President’s Letter

Welcome to NABA UIUC! We’re excited to support your growth and help you find your path in business. Whether you’re exploring majors or preparing for internships, there’s a place for you here. Join an event, meet the board, and let’s build together.
